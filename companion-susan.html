Susan (1963 to 1964)

Played by Carol Ann Ford

The mysterious granddaughter of the Doctor, Susan was one of the first companions to travel in the TARDIS. Posing as a student at Coal Hill School in London it is her unique character that caused Ian and Barbara to be drawn into the world of the Time Lords.

Susan's adventures lasted for approximately five stories before she chose to stay in 22nd Century London to help rebuild after a Dalek invasion. She would later make an appearance in the anniversary story The Five Doctors.
