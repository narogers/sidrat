New Series

Excitement began to stir with rumors that Doctor Who was finally returning to the airwaves in around 2004. Reimagined by Russell T. Davies the new series is mostly faithful to the classic but updates the series for the 21st Century.

So far three Doctors have played the role. With a rotating cast of companions and a proper budget for special effects this version stands up as one of the premier science fiction shows today.
