Other materials

In addition to books printed since the classic series went on hiatus Doctor Who has a long tradition in print. This include novelizations of classic stories, comic books, and spin offs from the main line.

Among the most well known are the Target novelizations. Written by contributors such as Terrance Dicks these short form novels take the Doctor's adventures to print. Sometimes faithful to the televised story, but often not, they can easily be found for those looking to build their own collection.

The Abominable Snowmen

A single blow from the giant, hairy paw smashes the explorer to the ground. Terrified, he flees from the monster's glowing eyes and savage fangs...

Why are the peaceful Yeti now spreading death and destruction? And what is the secret behind the glowing cave on the mountain?

When DOCTOR WHO discovers that a long-dead friend is still alive, he knows why his visit to the lonely Himalayan monastery has led to a struggle to save the Earth!
