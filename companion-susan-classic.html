Classic Series (1963 to 1966)

Susan would appear in the first ten serials of the original series. Choosing to leave to marry a Dalek resistance fighter at the end of The Dalek Invasion of Earth her departure would be the first of many bittersweet moments for Doctors over the next 40 years.

Older and wiser, Susan would reappear for the 100th serial celebration The Five Doctors in the early 1980s.
