First Doctor (1963 to 1966)

Played by William Hartnell

The measuring stick by which all future Doctors would be compared, William Hartnell was the first actor cast in the role of the mysterious Time Lord. Already an established performer he played the part of a grumpy old man hiding from his past.

William Hartnell eventually stepped down due to failing health in early 1966. This paved the way for the first of many regenerations and opportunities for writers and actors to reinvent the role throughout the next half century.
