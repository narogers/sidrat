Virgin Missing Adventures

Following on the popularity of the New Adventures the Missing Adventures let writers create stories for previous Doctors. Some took place between television episodes while others were standalone.

Published monthly beginning in 1994 the series lasted until Virgin's license expired in the middle of 1997. In all a total of 33 books were published as part of the series.
